# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-03-28_

## Overview

Creator Stats App is a private developer application used to access and process analytics data from official platform APIs for accounts owned by, controlled by, or explicitly authorized to the operator of the app.

The app is currently intended for internal analytics, testing, and development workflows.

## Data we may access

Depending on the platform integration and granted permissions, the app may access:

- basic account or profile information
- public account statistics such as follower counts, likes, views, or content totals
- content-level statistics for videos, posts, tracks, or similar creator content
- technical authorization data required to maintain API access, such as access tokens or refresh tokens

The app is designed to request only the minimum data needed for the analytics workflow being tested or used.

## How data is used

Data accessed by the app may be used to:

- fetch creator/account statistics from official APIs
- store timestamped local snapshots for comparison over time
- generate private summaries, digests, or change reports
- maintain or debug analytics integrations

The data is not collected for resale, advertising, or profiling unrelated third parties.

## Data sharing

Data accessed by the app is not sold or shared publicly by default.

Data may be stored in private local files, private infrastructure, or development environments controlled by the operator of the app. Data may also be processed through platform APIs strictly for the app’s intended analytics use.

## Data retention

Analytics snapshots and related metadata may be retained for as long as needed for development, testing, and historical comparison, unless deletion is requested or the data is no longer needed.

Authorization credentials and tokens are stored separately from public application files and are intended to be handled as confidential secrets.

## Security

Reasonable efforts are made to keep credentials and private analytics data restricted to the operator’s controlled environment. However, no system can be guaranteed to be perfectly secure.

## User rights and requests

If you are the owner of an authorized account and want to ask about access, revocation, correction, or deletion related to your data, contact the operator of the app using the contact method associated with the GitHub Pages site or repository where this policy is published.

## Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the app, its integrations, or platform requirements. The latest published version is the version that applies.
